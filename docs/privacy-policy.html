Milan Vivah - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25th October 2023

At Milan Vivah, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services. By using Milan Vivah, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

  1. Information We Collect

    2.

    We collect various types of information to deliver our matchmaking services effectively:

    Information Submitted by You

    When you register and use Milan Vivah, you provide us with personal data to help you find your ideal life partner. This information includes:

    • Name
    • Gender
    • Date of Birth
    • Contact Details
    • Educational Qualifications
    • Employment Details
    • Photos
    • Marital Status
    • Interests
    • Sensitive Personal Data, such as Identity Proofs

    If you opt for our premium services, you may also provide sensitive financial information, such as bank account details, debit/credit card numbers, or UPI.

    You may also submit testimonials, success stories, photos, and participate in surveys, contests, promotions, or events. Details shared with our customer care team may be used for monitoring, training, and service improvement.

    Additionally, you have the option to send profiles of interest to your contacts via WhatsApp. Your chats, messages, and published content are processed as part of our service.

    Information Not Directly Submitted by You

    We collect information about your activity on Milan Vivah, including login times, features you use, searches, clicks, pages visited, and interactions with other users, including exchanged messages.

    Device information, such as IP addresses, device IDs, device specifications, app settings, browser type, operating system, and identifiers related to cookies or other technologies, are also collected.

    Information We Receive from Others

    In addition to data you provide directly, we may receive information from your social media accounts if you choose to log in using services like Google SSO or Apple ID.

  2. How We Use Your Information

    3.

    We process your personal data for various purposes, including but not limited to:

    • Providing targeted matchmaking services.
    • Managing your Milan Vivah account.
    • Offering customer support.
    • Conducting research and analysis to enhance our services through AI and ML.
    • Communicating with you about services, promotions, or offers matching your interests via email, phone and other communication methods.
    • Recommending relevant matches and displaying your profile to other users.

  3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

    4.

    We take your privacy seriously and share your personal information only under the following circumstances:

    With Other Users

    To provide our services, we share information submitted by you with other users. Be cautious about what you share, and remember that you can control your privacy settings through the "setting/privacy option" page.

    With Our Service Providers and Partners

    We may engage third-party service providers for website and application development, hosting, maintenance, storage, payment processing, analysis, and other services. They access your information under strict confidentiality obligations and data protection measures.

    With Law Enforcement Agencies

    We will disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law or to protect our rights, the interests of other members, or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

  4. How to Access and Control Your Information

    5.

    You have tools to manage your information, and you have the right to access, rectify, update, or restrict your data. Members from the EU, UK, and California have specific rights related to their data.

    To review your information, request a copy of your personal data, withdraw consent, or opt-out of sharing your information with third-party service providers, please contact us.

    Note that certain requests may affect our ability to provide our services, as we may require specific information to ensure their functionality.

  5. Security of Your Information

    6.

    We implement security safeguards to protect your data, but no system is completely impenetrable. We follow industry standards to protect the information you submit, including sensitive data like credit card numbers. Access to your information is restricted to employees who need it for specific job functions.

  6. Retention of Personal Information

    7.

    We retain your data for as long as you use our services and as required by applicable laws. We delete or anonymize your information upon account deletion, except when necessary for legal obligations, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or business operations.

  7. Cookies and Tracking Tools

    8.

    We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your user experience. You can control their use through your browser settings.

  8. International Data Transfer

    9.

    We collect information globally, primarily storing it in India and the USA. Information collected within the European Economic Area ("EEA") may be transferred to countries outside of the EEA under data protection measures.

  9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

    10.

    11.
